How to Play — Battleship Bingo

Setup

Staff create a 10×10 board of drops/items. Each team gets one captain. Captains secretly place six ships: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (3), and Patrol Boat (2).

Gameplay

Players submit proof for a tile. When staff approve it, the tile fires an attack against the opposing fleet. Attacks are marked as hit or miss.

Winning

Sink all six enemy ships to win. If the event deadline arrives first, the team with the most ships sunk wins.
