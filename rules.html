Clan Rules

Keep the clan respectful, fair, and enjoyable for everyone.

1. Be respectful to clanmates and staff.
2. No scamming, harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.
3. Follow all event rules and submission requirements.
4. Do not leak secret challenge details.
5. Staff decisions are final when resolving disputes.
