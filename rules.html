Ironkin Standards

Clan Rules

Strength. Respect. Brotherhood. These are the standards that keep the forge burning.

Respect the Kin

Treat every member with courtesy and maturity. No harassment, trolling, or personal attacks. Disagreements happen — handle them without turning the chat into a toxic mess.

We Talk Like Adults Here

Ironkin isn’t another corner of the internet where you have to walk on eggshells. Mature discussion — even on sensitive topics — is welcome here.

You’re free to speak your mind, as long as it’s done respectfully. We draw the line at personal attacks, name-calling, and tantrums. If you can’t hold a civil conversation, you’ll be asked to drop it or take a break.

Disagreeing with someone or disliking the topic isn’t grounds to silence them. We’re built on strength, independence, and kinship — not fragility.

Uphold the Standards

We follow all Jagex & Discord ToS. No botting, RWT, bug abuse, account sharing, or other exploits. If it risks your account or the clan’s integrity, don’t do it.

Keep Our Space Focused

No unsolicited advertising for other clans, Discords, streams, services, or off-topic ventures (crypto, merch, etc.). Exceptions require Council approval.

Discord Is Non-Negotiable

Membership in the in-game clan requires active presence in this server. This is where events, announcements, and clan business happen.

Keep It Civil & Clean

NSFW content, hate speech, political bait, and inflammatory spam aren’t welcome here.

Debates and differing opinions are fine — shutting down conversations, derailing them with insults, or making the chat hostile is not.

Loot & Drops

All loot is FFA by default. If you choose to split, arrange it privately — staff will not enforce agreements. For competitions, drops must follow submission guidelines to count.

Represent the Forge

When you wear the Ironkin name, you represent all of us — in-game, on Discord, and in public spaces. Act in a way that strengthens, not weakens, our reputation.
